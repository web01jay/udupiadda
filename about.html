About Us

Welcome to Udupi Adda


Experience Authentic South Indian Flavors in Canada

At Udupi Adda, we bring the rich culinary traditions of South India to your table, right here in Canada. Our menu is a celebration of the vibrant flavors, aromatic spices, and time-honored recipes that make South Indian cuisine truly unique.

Our Story

Udupi Adda is more than just a restaurant—it's a journey back to the roots of traditional South Indian cooking. Named after the coastal town of Udupi, renowned for its vegetarian cuisine, our restaurant honors this heritage with every dish we serve. Whether you're a dosa enthusiast or new to South Indian food, you'll find something to love in our extensive menu.

Our Menu

From the crisp, golden dosas served with an array of chutneys and sambar to the comforting warmth of idlis and vadas, our dishes are prepared with the freshest ingredients and authentic spices. We offer a variety of options to cater to every palate—vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes are all available.

Why Choose Udupi Adda?

Authenticity: Our chefs are experts in South Indian cuisine, using traditional methods to ensure every dish tastes just like it does back home.
Freshness: We prioritize fresh, locally sourced ingredients to bring you the best in quality and flavor.
Diverse Options: With a menu full of diverse options, there's something for everyone—whether you're in the mood for a hearty meal or a light snack.

Visit Us

Located in the heart of Langley, Udupi Adda offers a cozy and welcoming environment where you can enjoy a meal with family, friends, or colleagues. Join us for a dining experience that’s not just about food, but also about celebrating the rich culture of South India.
